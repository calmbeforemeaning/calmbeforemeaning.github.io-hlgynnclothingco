Our Story

HLGYNN Clothing was born from a love for Iloilo and the vibrant Hiligaynon culture. We believe that language is more than words—it's identity, humor, and soul.

Every design celebrates the phrases we grew up with, the expressions that make us laugh, and the sayings that remind us of home. Whether you're from Iloilo or visiting for the first time, our pieces help you carry a piece of this beautiful culture with you.

"Basta galing sa Iloilo, quality!" — If it's from Iloilo, it's quality!
